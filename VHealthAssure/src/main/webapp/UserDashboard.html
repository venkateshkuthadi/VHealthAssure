Health Insurance

Health insurance is a plan that helps you pay for medical expenses when you or your family fall sick, get injured, or need surgery. Instead of paying the full hospital bill from your pocket, the insurance company pays according to the coverage chosen in your policy.

A good health insurance plan can cover doctor visits, lab tests, medicines, room rent, ICU charges, surgeries, daycare procedures and critical illnesses. It also protects your savings from unexpected medical emergencies and gives peace of mind, so you can focus on recovery instead of worrying about money.

Why Do I Need a Health Insurance Policy?

Health insurance is more than just a formality. It protects your savings, helps you manage long-term illnesses, and makes quality medical care easier to afford.

Rising Medical Costs

Hospital bills, surgeries, and tests are becoming expensive every year. A health insurance policy helps you manage these growing costs without breaking your savings.

Chronic Disease Management

Conditions like diabetes, heart disease, and hypertension need regular check-ups and medicines. Insurance supports your long-term treatment.

Financial Security

Instead of paying huge medical bills at once, your policy covers most of the expenses. This protects your emergency fund and keeps your financial plans on track.

Comprehensive Health Coverage

From hospitalization and diagnostics to pre and post-hospitalisation care, a good policy offers wide coverage for you and your family.

How Can I Buy a Medical Insurance Plan?

Follow these simple online steps to choose the right health insurance plan and get your policy instantly on email and WhatsApp.

Takes approx. 5–10 minutes
1
Step 1

Choose who you want to cover

Select the member or number of members to be covered under the policy – self, spouse, children, parents, or entire family.

2
Step 2

Enter basic details

Provide details such as date of birth, mobile number, email ID and city / PIN code so that we can show you the right plans and premium.

3
Step 3

Compare plans & coverage

Review available health insurance plans, check room rent, cashless hospital network, add-ons and waiting periods before selecting a plan.

4
Step 4

Fill medical & nominee details

Enter health declarations (if any illnesses), height & weight and nominee information so that your policy is issued correctly.

5
Step 5

Upload / verify documents

Upload soft copies of KYC documents such as Aadhaar / PAN / Passport as requested. In some cases, video KYC or tele-underwriting may be required.

6
Step 6

Pay premium & get policy instantly

Choose your preferred payment mode, complete the payment securely and download your e-policy. A copy will also be shared on your email / WhatsApp.
